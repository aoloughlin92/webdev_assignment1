Basic Climb

First we need to learn how to do the basic climb, also known as the french climb.

  • Place your right hand up high.
  • Right leg wraps outside to inside.
  • Bend the knee and flex the foot.
  • Join hands together.
  • Step left foot on top of right.
  • Push your feet forward to an L position.
  • Bend the elbows and stand.
